# Research Analyzer Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

The **Research Analyzer** project is committed to fostering an open, inclusive,
and respectful community. We aim to provide a harassment-free experience for
everyone involved, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender
identity and expression, level of experience, education, socioeconomic status,
nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and
orientation.

As maintainers and contributors, we pledge to act and interact in ways that
contribute to a welcoming, professional, and collaborative environment.

---

## Standards of Behavior

### Expected Behavior
Participants are expected to:
- Show empathy, respect, and courtesy toward others.
- Use professional, inclusive, and constructive language.
- Be open to different viewpoints and experiences.
- Provide and accept constructive feedback respectfully.
- Prioritize the health and growth of the community.

### Unacceptable Behavior
Unacceptable behaviors include, but are not limited to:
- Harassment or discrimination of any kind.
- Offensive comments related to personal characteristics.
- Sexualized language, imagery, or unwelcome advances.
- Trolling, insults, or personal attacks.
- Threats, intimidation, stalking, or sustained harassment.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Any conduct that is inappropriate in a professional or collaborative setting.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project-related spaces, including:
- GitHub repositories (issues, pull requests, discussions, code reviews).
- Project communication channels (email or future platforms).
- Public spaces where an individual represents **Research Analyzer**.

---

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for:
- Clarifying acceptable standards of behavior.
- Investigating reports of violations fairly and promptly.
- Taking appropriate corrective actions when necessary.

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this Code of
Conduct and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation as needed.

---

## Reporting Violations

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please
report it to the project maintainer:

- Open an issue on the repository with the title  
  **"Confidential: Code of Conduct Violation"**,  
  or
- Contact the maintainer directly via GitHub.

All reports will be handled with discretion and confidentiality. When reporting,
please include:
- A description of the incident.
- When and where it occurred.
- Any relevant context or evidence (if available).

---

## Enforcement Process

Upon receiving a report:
1. The maintainer will review the information provided.
2. A determination will be made regarding the severity of the violation.
3. Appropriate action will be taken, ranging from a warning to removal from the
   project.

Decisions will be made in good faith with the goal of maintaining a safe and
productive environment.
